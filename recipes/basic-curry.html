Basic curry

Description

This is a basic vegeterian curry recipe for anyone regardless of your cooking skills.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups brown jasmine rice, rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 small onion chopped
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 3 carrots
  • 3 tablespoons Red Thai paste
  • 1 can regular coconut milk
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the water in a large pot and cook the rice according to the instruction on the package.
  2. Pour oil in the wok and add onion and garlic. Start frying and stir regularly to not burn the ingredients
  3. Add the carrots as well as the rest of vegetables, water and cook slowly
  4. Add coconut milk and the Red Thai paste and continue cooking for the next 15 minutes
  5. Turn off the oven and leave the dish for 30 minutes so that the spices intensify the taste
  6. Serve rice in deep plates and pour it with our delicious curry sauce
  7. Bon appetit!
